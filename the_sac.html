Welcome to the cyber world

About the cyber world

The cyber world refers to the virtual space created by computer networks, where users can interact, share information, and engage in various activities. It encompasses the internet, intranets, and other digital communication platforms.

What we will learn in this course:

  1. Introduction to the cyber world and its components
  2. Cybersecurity and online safety
  3. Cybercrime and its impact on society
  4. Cyber ethics and responsible online behavior
  5. Emerging technologies in the cyber world
  6. Future trends and challenges in the cyber world
